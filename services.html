BUTTERCUP'S CLEANING SERVICE
SERVICES
  • BATHROOMS
    • Sinks: Wipe down counter (around the sink, behind the faucet), wipe down inside of sink
    • Vanity Counter: Wipe down counters and mirror
    • Tub/shower: Clean inside shower and tub as well as tile walls, clean shower doors inside and outside
    • Toilets: Clean inside bowl, seat, lid, tank, and all around whole toilet bowl
    • Mirros: Wipe all mirrors down (with Windex)
    • Floors/walls: Sweep and mop floors, wipe walls down if walls are tiles, wipe glass on light bulbs
    • Trash: Take trash out and put fresh bag in can
  • BEDROOMS
    • Dust furniture (end tables, knick-knacks, pictures, etc.)
    • Sweep and mop (If hard floor or tile)
    • Vacuum (if carpet)
    • Make beds (as needed)
    • Straighten up
    • Wipe fan blades and globes (lights)
    • Clean mirrors (all)
  • KITCHENS
    • Sink: Make sure dishes are washed, dried, and put away, wipe down counters throughout the whole kitchen, wipe down inside of sink, wipe down faucet
    • Refrigerator/freezer: Wipe down outside
    • Stove: Wipe down top, inside, and outside of oven, wipe down above fan and light (above stove), wipe down front of stove and oven, wipe down the back of stove and wall behind stove
    • Floors: Sweep and mop floors, wipe down cabinet doors throughout entire kitchen
    • Dishwasher: If there is one in kitchen, make sure dishwasher is empty and clean inside and outside, dishes put away
  • FAMILY ROOMS
    • Dust furniture (coffee and end tables, knick-knacks, pictures, etc.)
    • Sweep and mop (If hard floor or tile)
    • Vacuum (if carpet)
    • Straighten up
    • Wipe fan blades and globes (lights)
